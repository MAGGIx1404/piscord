<h2>✍️ Text & Writing</h2>

<p>
  Start writing naturally. This editor supports clean, distraction-free text for journaling,
  planning, or long-form writing.
</p>

<h2>✅ Lists & Checklists</h2>

<p>
  Lists are great for tasks, habits, and quick notes.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Daily priorities</li>
  <li>Ideas to explore</li>
  <li>Things to revisit later</li>
</ul>

<p>You can also create ordered lists:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Define the goal</li>
  <li>Break it into steps</li>
  <li>Execute consistently</li>
</ol>
